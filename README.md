---

## What is B2A?

B2A — Business-to-Agent — is a new model of software delivery.
While every platform builds AI tools for humans, Byzant builds 
for agents. Retail traders are the sponsors. Their AI agents 
are the operators. Byzant is the infrastructure layer 
connecting them to premium capabilities they could not 
otherwise access or afford.

---

## The Problem

Hedge funds have entire engineering teams building custom agent 
infrastructure — real-time data feeds, sentiment engines, risk 
modules, and dark pool monitors. The retail trader has none of 
this.

Even when tools exist, retail traders cannot use them. Setting 
up an Alpaca API connection, configuring Claude with trading 
skills, writing strategy prompts, and scheduling bots requires 
significant technical knowledge. The traders who need these 
tools most are the ones least equipped to build them.

Emotion is the #1 reason retail traders lose money — not lack 
of knowledge, but fear, greed, FOMO, and revenge trading.

---

## The Solution

Byzant is the App Store for AI trading agents. A 
protocol-native marketplace where agents discover, request, and 
consume capability upgrades — with humans as the approving 
layer. Built on MCP (Model Context Protocol), the standard AI 
agents already speak. Every module is pre-built, pre-connected, 
and agent-ready. One click to activate. No code required.

---

## How It Works

1. Trader signs up and connects their Alpaca brokerage account 
   in one click
2. Agent discovers available capability modules in the 
   marketplace
3. Trader activates modules — no setup, no configuration 
   required
4. Agent runs strategies, monitors positions, surfaces 
   decisions
5. Human reviews and approves — you are always the arbiter

## Marketplace Modules

**Smart Money & Whale Tracking**
- Whale Tracker — unusual options flow, dark pool monitoring, 
  volume anomaly alerts
- Congressional Tracker — politician trade disclosures via 
  Capitol Trades API, auto copy trading

**Automated Strategy Bots**
- Trailing Stop Bot — configurable trailing stops, ladder buy 
  rules, market-hours scheduling
- Wheel Strategy Bot — automated cash-secured puts and covered 
  calls, 50% profit early exit
- Copy Trading Bot — mirrors top-performing politicians and 
  institutional whales automatically

**Risk Agent (Pro)**
- Always-on risk enforcement — daily loss limits, position 
  sizing, correlation scanning, drawdown recovery
- Requires conscious human approval to override any rule
- The institutional risk desk retail traders never had

**Data Feeds**
- Real-time market data, news and sentiment, options flow, 
  dark pool activity, macro indicators

**Analytical Tools**
- Technical analysis engines, pattern recognition, volatility 
  calculators, backtesting modules

**Execution Layer**
- Alpaca one-click connection, paper and live trading, order 
  routing, trade journaling

**Agent Memory**
- Persistent strategy memory, trader preference storage, 
  market regime memory
